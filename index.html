How Nails by Parmeet Came Into Existence

During the quiet chaos of lockdown, when the world stood still, I found my spark in beauty and self-expression. I immersed myself in the world of nails — studying, practicing, and perfecting. With a certification in hand and dreams in my eyes, I built Nails by Parmeet: a passion turned into artistry, one brushstroke at a time.
